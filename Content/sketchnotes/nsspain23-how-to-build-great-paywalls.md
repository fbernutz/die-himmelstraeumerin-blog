## Detailed image description of the sketchnote

- A Paywall is a sales pitch for your product and converts users to customers
- but it's not a one-size-fits-all
- Best practices? ...it depends.

### Things to try

1. Timing & Placement
2. Design & Copy
3. Pricing & Offers
4. Experimentation

### 1. Timing

- Hard vs Soft Paywall
- Make it accessible
- Measure how many users see the paywall

### 2. Copy & Design

- Describe benefits
- Call to action
- avoid walls of text
- be transparent

### 3. Pricing

- experiment with packages
- offer discounts
- preselect package
- regional specific prices

### 4. Experiment

1. Experiment
2. Craft a thesis
3. Use results
4. Rinse and Repeat
